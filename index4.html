FESTIVAL

The Ganesh celebration just took place on campus.

Just a few days ago, our hostel came alive with the vibrant festivities of Ganesh Chaturthi, a celebration dedicated to Lord Ganesha. The atmosphere was filled with joy, colors, and a sense of togetherness as everyone eagerly participated in the preparations.
The celebration began with the installation of a beautifully adorned idol of Lord Ganesha in a decorated corner of the common area. We all contributed to the decoration, using flowers, lights, and colorful rangoli designs that added a festive touch.
The sight of the idol, surrounded by vibrant decorations, brought a sense of peace and happiness to our space.
